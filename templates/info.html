What is Skin Saviour?

Skin Saviour is an AI-powered application that uses advanced Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) technology to analyze skin lesion images and provide cancer probability assessments. It's designed to assist in early detection and screening.

How It Works

  1. Upload Image: Take or upload a clear photo of a skin lesion
  2. AI Analysis: Our CNN model analyzes the image features
  3. Get Results: Receive cancer probability percentage and risk level
  4. Take Action: Follow recommendations based on results

Best Practices

  • Use good lighting when taking photos
  • Ensure the image is clear and in focus
  • Take photos from multiple angles if possible
  • Document changes over time
  • Combine AI screening with professional exams

When to See a Doctor

Consult a dermatologist immediately if you notice:

  • New or changing moles or spots
  • Asymmetrical lesions
  • Irregular borders
  • Multiple colors in one lesion
  • Lesions larger than 6mm
  • Rapid growth or changes
